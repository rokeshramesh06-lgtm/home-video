# House Color And Furniture Plan

This set applies a warm, finished-home look to your house using soft painted walls, lighter flooring, and full furniture concepts.

## Wall Colors

- Main hall and stair lobby: Warm ivory `#F2E8D8`
- Upper hallway and passage: Soft greige `#E7DED3`
- Bedrooms: Warm white `#F7F2EA`
- Lounge areas: Sand beige `#E9DFD2`
- Exterior/terrace walls: Exterior off-white `#F3F0E8`

## Furniture Direction

- Ground floor hall: Sofa seating, TV unit, side table, feature lighting
- Stair lobby: Console table, accent lighting, clean stone look
- Upper hallway: Slim console, wall art, soft ceiling lights
- Bedrooms: Bed, side table, lamp, wardrobe, curtains
- Lounge: Low sofa, accent chair, soft decor, warm lighting
- Terrace: Outdoor seating, planters, relaxed resort-style finish
